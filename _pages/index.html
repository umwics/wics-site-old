University of Manitoba Computer Science Lounge

As you may have seen in the CSSA slack, WICS, .devClub, and the CSSA have create a discord server! We wanted to create a space where students can come and hang out in a casual manner while the university is closed. We would like everyone interested in joining to fill out this form to get an invite.

https://forms.gle/HSinscg1aStKjQR4A

The reason for the form is to keep the server a place for current and past CS students only, and to help us keep track of who's coming and going. This will help us keep the server a friendly and safe environment where you can chat about school, personal projects, or just keep in touch with other CS students. We look forward to seeing you there!

We are a group of University of Manitoba students who support women in technology. All are welcome to join!
